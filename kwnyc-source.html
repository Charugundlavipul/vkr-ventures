Get To Know Us

Keller Williams NYC is a full-service, luxury real estate brokerage representing clients in a broad spectrum of classes, including residential sales and leasing, new development, commercial, and retail. Keller Williams NYC is the New York City master franchise of Keller Williams.

The office was formed in 2011 with downtown and uptown offices and relaunched as one market center in July 2020 under new ownership. Since its inception, Keller Williams NYC has redefined the real estate business, modernizing and advancing the industry by fostering a culture of partnership in which clients and listings are represented in a collaborative environment with our agents.
